Directions

The conference venue is located at the University of Oulu (Linnanmaa campus), north of the city center. The university is easily reached from the city center by frequent local buses, with typical travel times of around 15–25 minutes. Most hotels, restaurants, and services are located in the city center.

Train (VR)

Long-distance trains in Finland are operated by VR. You can travel to Oulu either from Helsinki city center or from Helsinki Airport (typically with a rail transfer in the Helsinki area). Journey times vary, so please check schedules when planning.

Timetables and tickets: vr.fi (or directly: VR timetables).

From Oulu Train Station, the city center is a short walk away.

Local buses (OSL)

Local buses are operated by Oulun seudun liikenne (OSL / Oulu Regional Transport).

  • Route planner & real-time timetables: OSL Route Planner and Timetables & routes
  • There are frequent bus connections between the city center and the University of Oulu (Linnanmaa campus) (for example, bus lines 1, 2, 3, 5, and 8). We recommend using the route planner.

By air (Oulu Airport, OUL)

Oulu Airport (OUL) is served by frequent flights from Helsinki and a direct flight from Riga. It is located about 15–25 minutes from the city centre by bus or car (depending on traffic). Airport arrival and departure information can be checked at: Flights at Oulu Airport.

Airport → city centre
  • Local buses: check times in the OSL Route Planner.
  • Taxis and car rental are available at the airport on arrival.

Hotels and accommodation

Most hotels and other accommodation options are located in the city center of Oulu. The conference venue at the University of Oulu (Linnanmaa campus) is not within the hotel area and is typically reached by public transport.

Participants are advised to book accommodation in the city center, where there is the widest selection of hotels, restaurants, and services, and to travel to the university by local bus.
